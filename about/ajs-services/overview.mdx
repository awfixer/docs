## What is the Purpose of AJ Services?

AJ Services is our cost offsetting divsion focused on high value sales focused enterprise clients. We have a goal of providing cheap easily afforded consumer products and so to help us achieve this goal we created AJ Services so that we can serve large, high value clients and charge large, high value prices while continuing to provide cheap quality services to our consumer divisions and open source as many things as possible.

<Info>
  Keep in mind that the minimum price for something via AJ Services is \$1000 a month
</Info>
