## Who is AWFixer Holding?

AWFixer Holding is the parent company of AJ Services, AWFixer's Lounge and AWFixer and Friends. It is a privately held Limited Liability Corporation owned in its entirity by AWFixer and jinx.

## What does AWFixer Holding do?

AWFixer Holding is the legal business for the companies we own and provides us with a centeralized way to manage the companies. The check that is signed for our employees at the end of every month is from AWFixer Holding LLC and our taxes are billed out to AWFixer Holding LLC rather than any of the companies we own.
