## What is AWFixerVPN

AWFixerVPN is a combination of a handful of services:

- a Virtual Private Network
- a DNS Sink for blocking ADs and domains that track you/have been blacklisted from being accessed via our services
- a series of anti tracking services that help prevent your internet traffic from being tracked and linked to you
- a series of bypass tools for internet blockages.
