## What is AWFixerNet

AWFixerNet is a network that hosts and covers products like:

- AWFixer Cloud
- AWFixer Network
- AWFixer VPN
- SimpleCMS

and provides their security, survellence resistance and ensures they are not spammed, abused, scraped or used for malicious purposes.
